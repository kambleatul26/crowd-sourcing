WHAT IS CROWDFUNDING IN WOMEN?

Pooling in small amount of funds from a large number of people online to support projects working to create a quality life for women.

Using the power of Internet to inspire people to create equal opportunities for women and spread the message of equality.

"Raise funds to fuel a women led startup in their early stage of business."

“Love handicrafts from Gujarat? Fund our female artisans to help them display their talent and provide a platform for them to shine.”

"Provide women working together in SHGs with adequate funds to help them achieve a life of self-sufficiency.“
